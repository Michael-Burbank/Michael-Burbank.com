I actively contribute to open-source projects, collaborating with the global developer community to build impactful, scalable technologies. My passion for coding and design is evident in my accomplishments, which include developing web applications, mobile apps, and websites that prioritize user experience and accessibility. I utilize Agile methodologies, using iterative development and collaboration to deliver high-quality software that meets stakeholder needs. I place stakeholder feedback at the forefront of my process, ensuring that every solution aligns with user needs and business goals. My contributions to projects like browser extensions such as "Dark Reader" reflect my commitment to creating meaningful, user-centric software while focusing on accessibility and inclusivity.

As an ADHD coder, I share my personal experiences, strategies, and tools to help others navigate the challenges of focus, time management, and productivity in the tech industry. From leveraging Notion to streamline tasks to using noise-canceling headphones and distraction-blocking apps, my innovative approaches resonate with coders worldwide. My engaging content on platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and Lemon8 fosters a supportive community for developers with ADHD.

Outside of coding, I am an avid adventurer who enjoys flying, skydiving, hiking, and exploring the outdoors with my dog. As a passionate music enthusiast and active community volunteer, I combine my love for technology, personal growth, and creativity to inspire and empower others in the ever-evolving field of software development.
